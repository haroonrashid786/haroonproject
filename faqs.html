FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request pick-up trucks and movers?

You fill in all the required details for your move request such as where to pick up and where to deliver, what needs to be transported, how many movers will be necessary, and whether it is necessary to disassemble and assemble, and if you also need packaging. And set your price.

How can I set a price if I haven’t taken this route?

We provide you with an estimate of the average cost of the service in the market, upon your request. Nevertheless, you are free to set your price for this service, based on the average market price that we provide.

Can I pay by card?

When creating a request, indicate whether you wish to pay in cash or by an online transfer.

How do I complete requests in a freight vehicle? (partners)

To receive access to requests, you need to place an ad in “Available Vehicles”. Describe your vehicle in detail and indicate the price of the service that you offer. After you add your offer, you can start sending requests to clients. You will be able to call a passenger after they confirm a request.

Whether you’re moving home, relocating your business or simply need a large item moved from one location to another, OneMove application perfectly tailored to your needs.
